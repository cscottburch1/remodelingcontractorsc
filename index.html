Residential Remodeling in Upstate South Carolina

Remodeling Contractors SC builds practical home projects in Upstate South Carolina. We build garages, room additions, decks, aluminum screened enclosures, detached ADUs, and lake cabin porch enclosures. We serve Mauldin, Simpsonville, Fountain Inn, Gray Court, Owings, Enoree, Woodruff, Laurens, Clinton, Joanna, Greer, and Greenwood.

Our process is simple. First, we review your goals, your site, and local code rules. Next, we confirm scope and budget. Then we coordinate permits, structure, and scheduling. This keeps your project on track from consultation to final walkthrough.

Core Service Categories

  • Detached and attached garage construction
  • Room additions including bump-outs and second-story expansions
  • Deck construction with covered and composite options
  • Aluminum framed screened porch and patio enclosures
  • Detached ADUs and guest-house style structures where permitted
  • Lake cabin screened porch enclosures near Lake Greenwood

Most homeowners start 2-4 months before construction. This gives time for scope, structural planning, permits, and scheduling. For permit reference, review South Carolina LLR guidance and your local building department requirements.
